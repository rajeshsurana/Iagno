Iagno

Welcome to Iagno game. This is the upgrade to the existing game built in with Linux Operating systems.

Objective of Iagno is to make as many discs of your color as possible. Game starts with four discs two for each player already criss-crossed in the center. Black plays first. Each time you have to sandwich one or more lines of your opponent's discs between your own discs. Then sandwiched discs change color and become yours. Then game continues until neither player can legally sandwich any more lines of discs.
